# Exercises

1. $$
	\begin{equation*}
	\int_{0}^{2\pi}
	\frac{d\theta}{
	A \cos^2 \theta
	+ 2B \cos \theta \sin \theta
	+ C \sin^2 \theta
	},
	\qquad A > 0,\; AC > B^2
	\end{equation*}
	$$





2. $$
	\begin{equation*}
	\int_{0}^{a}
	\left(
	a^{\frac{2}{3}} - x^{\frac{1}{3}}
	\right)^{\frac{3}{2}}
	\, dx
	\end{equation*}
	$$





3.  Find the limit of the sequence
	$$
	\begin{equation*}
	\lim_{n \to \infty}
	\frac{1}{\ln n}
	\int_{0}^{\pi/2}
	\frac{\sin^2 (n x)}{\sin x}\, dx.
	\end{equation*}
	$$
